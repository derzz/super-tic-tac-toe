Instructions

Super Tic Tac Toe is an advanced version of the classic Tic Tac Toe game. The objective is to win more small boards to win the overall game.

  • Each cell in the main 3x3 grid contains a smaller 3x3 Tic Tac Toe board.
  • Players take turns playing on the smaller boards.
  • The position of your move in the smaller board determines the next smaller board your opponent must play in.
  • If a player is sent to a board that has already been won or is full, they can choose any board for their move.
  • Winning a smaller board is achieved by getting three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  • The game is won by winning three smaller boards in a row on the main board.

To win the game, strategize to control the flow of the game and force your opponent into disadvantageous positions.
